🍫 Brownie Recipe

🥣 Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs + water
  • Powdered sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract

🧑‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Heat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix eggs, water, and oil.
  3. Add cocoa powder, sugar, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour into a pan and bake for 25–30 minutes.
